Influenza (Flu)

Symptoms: Fever, cough, sore throat, body aches, fatigue, headache, runny or stuffy nose.

Precautions: Get vaccinated annually, wash hands frequently, avoid close contact with sick individuals, cover mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Symptoms: Often asymptomatic, but severe headaches, shortness of breath, nosebleeds, and chest pain may occur in advanced stages.

Precautions: Maintain a healthy diet low in sodium, exercise regularly, manage stress, monitor blood pressure regularly, and follow prescribed medications.

Hypertension (HBP)

Diabetes Mellitus

Symptoms: Increased thirst and urination, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, blurred vision, slow-healing wounds, and frequent infections

Precautions: Monitor blood sugar levels regularly, maintain a healthy diet, exercise regularly, take prescribed medications, and avoid smoking

Diabetes Mellitus (DM)

Coronary Artery Disease

Symptoms: Chest pain or discomfort (angina), shortness of breath, nausea, fatigue, irregular heartbeat

Precautions: Maintain a healthy diet low in saturated fats and cholesterol, exercise regularly, manage stress, quit smoking, and control blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Coronary Artery Disease

Asthma

Symptoms: Wheezing, coughing, shortness of breath, chest tightness.

Precautions: Avoid triggers such as allergens and pollutants, use inhalers as prescribed, maintain a clean indoor environment, and have an asthma action plan.

Asthma

Osteoporosis

Symptoms: Bone fractures (especially in the hip, spine, or wrist) with minimal trauma, loss of height over time, stooped posture, back pain.

Precautions: Consume a diet rich in calcium and vitamin D, engage in weight-bearing exercises, quit smoking, limit alcohol intake, and undergo bone density tests.

Osteoporosis

Dengue Fever

Symptoms: Sudden high fever, severe headaches, pain behind the eyes, severe joint and muscle pain, fatigue, nausea, vomiting, skin rash.

Precautions: Eliminate mosquito breeding sites, use mosquito repellents, wear long sleeves and pants, and seek medical attention for severe symptoms.

Dengue Fever

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, chronic cough, wheezing, frequent respiratory infections, chest tightness

Precautions: Avoid tobacco smoke and air pollutants, exercise regularly to improve lung function, practice breathing techniques, and adhere to prescribed medications.
